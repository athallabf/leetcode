## How to Use This
After solving a problem:
1. Identify the primary pattern/pattern(s) used
2. Check the box if this is your 1st, 2nd, or 3rd+ time seeing it
3. When you get 3+ checks, move it to your "internalized" patterns list
4. Review monthly: which patterns still feel new? Focus practice there.
